Leo 24/7 Techie since 2011

Charles received a sudden shock - all the Microsoft Office applications on Charles’ Windows 7 computer had simply stopped working! He was unable to edit documents and was unsure if all his work had been saved. With an upcoming fundraiser for his church, he was desperate to get back up and running.

He was getting an error message indicating that his Microsoft licenses had expired, but he did not know how to renew. He called Microsoft and had to endure hours on hold. He visited his local electronics shop. But to no avail.

Then Charles contacted 24/7 Techies. Leo was assigned to take care of the problem and patiently listened to what was going on. With approval from Charles, he was able to remotely ‘drive’ the Windows 7 computer. This allowed an accurate diagnosis of the problem.

Leo was able to unlock Office applications. It turned out that the licenses had not even expired! Leo then explained what the real problem was to Charles, and how he could take care of it himself if the issue was to recur.

Dyon 24/7 Techie since 2011

Mary Beth, a resident at a Maryland retirement community was looking to install Microsoft Office on her computer. But she was getting an ‘activation’ error! She took the computer to a local shop. Despite not being able to help, the local tech had charged a fee for the visit anyway!

Back home, Mary Beth found 247 Techies. Dyon was assigned to help her. Based on Marybeth’s responses to initial questions and a preliminary diagnosis, Dyon determined that Marybeth’s computer had been hacked! She had clicked on a link contained in a fraudulent email. Along with corrupting her email account, the fraudulent script had also started corrupting the operating system.

On hearing this, Mary Beth was understandably distraught. But Dyon was able to assure her that no files had been copied yet. Furthermore, he would remove the fraudulent scripts, upgrade her security software, and fortify her computer against further attacks.

Following this happy resolution, Mary Beth asked for the 247 Techies support icon to be installed on her computer so that she could get in touch with us at any time.

Ray 24/7 Techie since 2011

Jeanne had suddenly realized that all the files on her computer had somehow disappeared! She had already called the tech support concierge in her community, but was told to contact Microsoft. She called Microsoft, but was unable to speak to a human. She then called Geek Squad, and even visited local Best Buy, but neither were of help. Finally, she called 24/7 Techies, and within 60 seconds, our techie Ray was on the case.

Ray guided Jeanne through the remote connection process to diagnose the issue. He determined that the problem arose from a recent Windows update, but fortunately he was able to recover her lost files.

He explained to Jeanne what had happened and what precautions she could take to prevent such problems in the future. Jeanne was delighted with the support, and called it an outstanding experience.
